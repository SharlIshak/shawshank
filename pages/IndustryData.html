    OUR TEAMS IDEAL JOBS RANKED

    Software Developer, Cyber security Analyst, Data Scientist, Game Developer, Software Engineer Manager

    The demand jobs in the cyber security field are on-going and rapidly increasing due to security threats towards users, to ensure protection of computer systems and hardware against such threats, the demand is high especially for large corporations such as Facebook, Google etc. Cyber security analysts prevent cyber-attacks on businesses and to avoid confidential and important loss. Next comes software developers which is a growing field due to many businesses and companies switching over to a technological approach, such as having a personal software relating to the business. Software developers are needed to develop such software's and thus a high demand in software developers. Next is data scientists with the evolving technology data analyst turn to computer systems and analytical management to provide a simplified and accurate results, lastly game developers who are also in the works of an evolving demand for the job, as the years come by technology rapidly improves and thus resulting in an improvement in games and the industry. Game developers have a mid-demand due to certain skills and requirements required to develop a game, the industry is very competitive compared to cyber security. The novice game developers might not have a very high demand due to lack of experience and skills compared to another game developer with much more experience in the field.
